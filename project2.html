Founded on the ideas that buildings can serve as a bridge between nature, culture and people.

The owner’s affection for a stone outcropping on her property inspired the design of this house. Conceived as a retreat nestled into the rock, the Pierre celebrates the materiality of the site.

From certain angles, the house—with its rough materials, encompassing stone, green roof, and surrounding foliage—almost disappears into nature.
