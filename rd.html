Revolución Democrática was born to face the fracture between citizens and the political system.

We call for profound changes to our political, economic and social system in the search for greater equality, representativeness and justice;

We have committed ourselves to the transformation of our society and started a work to constitute the movement as a real political alternative of democratic deepening and a new development model for Chile.

DIGITAL DEMOCRACY


I Implemented an electronic voting system to strengthen internal democracy. The system offers multiple layers of security, encryption, and recursion; together with a validation system using Identity Card through a specialized API to connect to the Civil Registry data. To date, more than 100 successful elections have been made using the system.
